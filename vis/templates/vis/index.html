Hello! Welcome to QuVis.
QuVis is a visualization platform for novices to develop the intuition behind creating quantum circuits for quantum computers.
To add gates to the circuit, click on the desired gate from the Add Gate panel.
Click on the Circuit Composer panel to apply the gate. The bars in front of each gate illustrate how the gate affects that qubitâ€™s final probability.
The State Probabilities panel lists all the possible states for this circuit and their respective probabilities.
Hover the slider on top of gates to see how these probabilities change over time. Good Luck!
